EcoQuest Game

Instructions:

In EcoQuest, your goal is to reach the end of the board. Click "Start EcoQuest" to begin your journey. Along the way, you'll land on spaces that challenge your environmental knowledge with a series of questions. Answer correctly to earn points and move forward, but if you answer incorrectly, you'll move back a space. Beware of the spaces marked 'x', you'll move back anywhere from one to three spaces. Have fun, and make the best choices to build a Greener World!
